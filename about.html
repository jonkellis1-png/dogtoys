About Me

Hello! My name is Jonathan Kellis. I'm 13 years old and currently attend Dexter Southfield School.

Recently, I adopted a puppy named Pooka who is only 2 months old. Spending time with Pooka inspired me to start this project: a small online shop where dog owners can buy fun and safe toys for their furry friends.
